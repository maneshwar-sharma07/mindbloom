Understanding
Depression

Depression can be debilitating, but there
is hope for recovery with the right
treatment and support.



What is Depression?

A mental health condition characterized by persistent feelings of sadness and loss of interest

  • persistent and persuaive
  • affects how you feel,think,and handle daily activities

Common Signs & Symptoms

  • Restlessness or nervousness
  • Persistent sadness
  • Loss of interest or pleasure
  • Changes in sleep
  • Fatigue

Potential causes

  • Chemical inbalances in brain
  • Genetics and family history
  • Chronic medical conditions

Steps Towards Feeling Better

  • Medication
  • Counselling
  • Physical exercises
  • Seeking therapy
