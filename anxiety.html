Understanding
Anxiety

Anxiety can be overwhelming, but with
the right tools, healing is possible.



What is Anxiety?

A mental health condition characterized by feelings of worry, fear, or apphension

  • Excessive and persistent
  • Can interfere with daily life
  • Often involves physical symptoms

Common Signs & Symptoms

  • Restlessness or nervousness
  • Increased Heart rate
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Irritability

Causes and Triggers

  • Stressful life events or traumas
  • Genetics and family history
  • Underlying health conditions

Want to Seek Help

Reach out to a mental health proffessional if anxiety is affecting your daily life or causing significant distress.
It's sometime important to reach-out because help is needed.
Don't suffer alone.Ask for help, trust the process, you will come out stronger.

Coping Strategies

  • Breathing exercises
  • Journaling
  • Talking to someone
  • Seeking therapy
